Ribeye Steak with Mayonaise

A beautiful steak served with mayo.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Ribeye Steak (Click Here for Recipe)
  • Jar of Store-bought Mayonaise

Instructions

  1. Follow instructions for cooking a ribeye steak.
  2. Add a spoon to the jar of mayo
  3. Serve ribeye steak beside the jar of mayo.

Congratulations! You did it. You served mayo with your steak.
