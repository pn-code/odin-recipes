Ribeye Steak with Chili

A beautiful steak served with canned chili.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Ribeye Steak (Click Here for Recipe)
  • Store-bought Chili in a can

Instructions

  1. Follow instructions for cooking a ribeye steak.
  2. Open the can of chili and put a spoon inside.
  3. Serve can of chili beside the steak.

Congratulations! You served a can of chili beside a Ribeye Steak.
