Ribeye Steak with Garlic

A beautiful steak that can be used to battle Dracula himself.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Ribeye Steak (Click Here for Recipe)
  • 4 cloves of store-bought peeled garlic

Instructions

  1. Follow instructions for cooking a ribeye steak.
  2. Put garlic cloves on the same plate as the steak.
  3. Serve.

Congratulations! You put some peeled garlic next to your Ribeye Steak.
