Ribeye Steak

A beautifully cooked ribeye steak that is seasoned to perfection. It is the definition of juicy perfection.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Grapeseed oil

Instructions

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Put the steak in the fridge for one hour or overnight.
  3. Take the steak out of the fridge.
  4. Leave at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  5. Heat up a stainless steel or cast-iron pan to maximum heat.
  6. Add some grapeseed oil to the pan.
  7. Cook steak for 90 seconds each side.
  8. Take out of pan and let rest for 5-10 minutes.
  9. Put on plate and serve.
